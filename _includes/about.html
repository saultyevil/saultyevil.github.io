I am currently a PhD student researching line formation in outflows in Tidal Disruption Events using Monte Carlo radiative transfer and ionization simulations. I currently attend the University of Southampton and am supervised by Christian Knigge and are part of the Next Generation Computational Modelling Centre for Doctoral Training (NGCM-CDT) at Southampton. During my PhD, and as part of the NGCM, worked with world-class HPC facilities using a range of programming languages and parallelisation/HPC frameworks, such as, but not limited to, CUDA, OpenMP and MPI. I have also attended multiple workshops on modern programming techniques and international astronomy conferences, where I have presented my work to the community.
